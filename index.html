Hi!
My name is Chris Carter and I've been apart of the autism community since 2011, I have dedicated myself to supporting individuals with autism, working extensively within school districts across Ventura County and Los Angeles County. My diverse experience includes roles such as a para-professional, Registered Behavior Technician (RBT), Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Therapist, special needs personal trainer, basketball coach, and in-home caregiver. These roles have equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of the unique needs and strengths of individuals with autism.

My coaching philosophy centers on "highlighting abilities" rather than focusing on disabilities. I firmly believe in emphasizing what can be accomplished, fostering an environment of empowerment and positivity. By concentrating on the strengths and potential of each individual, I aim to help them reach their fullest potential and lead fulfilling lives.

As a dedicated life coach for children and adults with autism, I leverage my extensive experience and specialized training to provide tailored support that meets the unique needs of each individual. My approach is centered on fostering personal growth and independence through structured, empathetic, and strengths-based coaching. By highlighting abilities rather than focusing on limitations, I empower my clients to achieve their fullest potential. I work closely with families, educators, and other support systems to create a cohesive and nurturing environment. Whether through personalized fitness programs, social skills training, or academic support, I am committed to enhancing the quality of life for my clients, promoting self-confidence, and facilitating meaningful progress in their personal and social development.
